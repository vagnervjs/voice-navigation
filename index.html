Voice Navigation

Shortcuts

  • 🎤 S: Start voice
  • 🛑 E: End voice
  • ⬆️ ↑: Move up
  • ⬇️ ↓: Move down
  • ↩️ ←: Rotate left
  • ↪️ →: Rotate right
  • ⬅️ H: Move left
  • ➡️ J: Move right
  • 🔼 N: Heading up
  • 🔽 M: Heading down

Voice Commands

  • 🚶 Forward / Go
  • ↩️ Back / Backward
  • ↰ Turn left
  • ↱ Turn right
  • ⬆️ Look up
  • ⬇️ Look down
  • 🧭 North / South
  • 🧭 East / West
  • 🛑 Stop (voice)
  • 💬 "Go forward"
